# TEXT COLORS

 RED | "\x1B[31m" | [RED](#rocket-RED)

 BLUE | "\x1B[34m" | [BLUE](#rocket-BLUE)
 MAGENTA | "\x1B[35m" | [MAGENTA](#rocket-MAGENTA)
 CYAN | "\x1B[36m" | [CYAN](#rocket-CYAN)
 WHITE | "\x1B[37m" | [WHITE](#rocket-WHITE)
