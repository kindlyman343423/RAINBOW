# TEXT COLORS

 USED VARIABLE | COLOR CODE | OUTPUT COLOR
---------------|------------|--------------
 green | "\x1B[2m" | green
 BLACK | "\x1B[30m" | BLACK
 RED | "\x1B[31m" | RED
 GREEN | "\x1B[32m" | GREEN
 YELLOW | "\x1B[33m" | YELLOW
 BLUE | "\x1B[34m" | BLUE
 MAGENTA | "\x1B[35m" | MAGENTA
 CYAN | "\x1B[36m" | CYAN
 WHITE | "\x1B[37m" | WHITE
